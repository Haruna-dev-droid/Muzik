About Us

SoundWave Records is a leading independent record label specializing
in Afro-fusion, R&B, and Hip-Hop. Founded in 2020, we are dedicated to
discovering and nurturing exceptional talent from Africa and the diaspora.
Our mission is to amplify diverse voices and bring innovative sounds to a
global audience through artist development, cutting-edge production, and
strategic marketing.
